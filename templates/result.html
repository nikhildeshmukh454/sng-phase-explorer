About the Model

Real gas mixtures cannot be described accurately by the ideal gas law. To capture intermolecular interactions and phase behaviour, thermodynamic Equations of State (EOS) are used.

This tool compares two important models:

  • Peng–Robinson EOS: Widely used cubic equation of state in petroleum and chemical engineering.
  • PC-SAFT EOS: Molecular-based EOS capable of modeling complex chain-like molecules with higher accuracy.

What Does the Phase Diagram Show?

  • Bubble Curve: liquid begins to vaporize.
  • Dew Curve: vapor begins to condense.
  • Two Phase Region: liquid and vapor coexist.
  • Critical Point: phases become indistinguishable.
